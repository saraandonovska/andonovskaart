About Me

The artist behind the canvas, 2026

"Every brushstroke carries a piece of emotion that words could never express, speaking the language my heart cannot. "

I'm Sara - an artist at heart and a Computer Science and Engineering student at FINKI. While technology teaches me how things work, painting teaches me how they feel.

Alongside my studies, I have followed my passion as a self-taught painter working mostly in acrylic and charcoal, drawn to experiment with mixed media and giving meanings to blank canvases. Since childhood, art has been my way of expressing emotions that words cannot fully describe.

My journey through visual arts, UX/UI design, and web development has shown me that the most powerful ideas are born where logic meets emotion, creating pieces that invites people to pause, feel, and remember.

Based in Skopje
Practice Acrylic · Charcoal · Mixed media
Started as soon as I could hold a pencil

Exhibition

"Impuls" — my first solo exhibition, held 3rd-9th March 2026 in Skopje.

Collection of 10 works created from moments when emotion overcomes routine and inner truth begins to surface. The exhibition explores the quiet yet powerful instant when feelings become impossible to ignore, transforming into color, texture, and form.

Honors & Awards

  • young age

    National & European Art Recognition

    Recipient of multiple awards in children's and youth art competitions organized by the City of Skopje and the European Union, reflecting a long-standing dedication to visual arts from an early age.

  • 2022

    British Embassy Art Competition Winner

    Won first place in an art competition organized by the British Embassy, with the winning painting featured on a public billboard in the center of Skopje.

  • 2023

    First Place – Skala Group Exhibition

    Awarded first place in both the Junior and Senior categories at the Skala School Group Exhibition (2023), recognizing artistic excellence across two age divisions.

  • 2023/24

    UX/UI+ Academy

    Received a scholarship to the UX/UI+ Academy (2023–2024) after earning first place at the Skala Group Exhibition, expanding my creative practice into digital design.

  • 2023

    Young Art Group Exhibition

    Selected to participate in the Young Art group exhibition organized by the Agency of Youth and Sports (December 2023), showcasing emerging young artists.
